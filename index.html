Weight Training

Also known as Strength Training or resistance training involves the performance of physical exerises that are designed to improve strength and endurance. It include body weight exercises,isumetrics and plyometrics.

General Fitness

It includes those exercises that increase the ability to perform daily activities without getting exhausted easily. It includes exercise such as plank, running, squard and chin ups etc.

Functional Training

The gym focuses on execises that mimic real-life movements and activities. It aims to improve your overall Strenght, flexibility, balance, coordination and endurance to enhance your ability to perform daily tasks efficiently and reduce the risk of injuries. Common functionl exercises includes, push-ups, pull-ups and exercises using stability balls, resistance bands and kettlebells. The goal is to train multiple muscle groups simultaneously and engage the core muscles to improve stability and functional movement patterns.
